Coconut Oil

Manufacturing Process

8-Step Production Process

Every batch of NaturGuard is crafted through a precise, quality-controlled process to ensure consistency and safety.

01

Raw Material Sourcing

Certified organic neem leaves, cold-pressed coconut oil, pharmaceutical-grade menthol crystals, and natural odor neutralizers are sourced from verified suppliers.

02

Inspection & Quality Check

All raw materials undergo rigorous lab testing for purity, potency, and contamination before entering the production floor.

03

Neem Extraction

Active compounds are extracted from neem leaves using a cold-press solvent-free method to preserve azadirachtin potency and bioavailability.

04

Coconut Oil Refinement

Virgin coconut oil is gently filtered and deodorized at low temperatures to retain natural fatty acids and moisturising properties.

05

Blending & Emulsification

Neem extract, coconut oil, menthol, and odor neutralizer are precisely blended in controlled ratios using cold-process emulsification for a stable, smooth formulation.

06

Stability & Efficacy Testing

The blended formula undergoes accelerated stability testing, dermatology patch tests, and live mosquito repellency trials to validate performance claims.

07

Filling & Sealing

Approved batches are filled into sterilised amber glass bottles using automated, air-free filling machines to prevent oxidation and contamination.

08

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Products are packaged in 100% recyclable, biodegradable packaging materials with tamper-evident seals, batch codes, and manufacturing dates.

Usage Guide

How to Use NaturGuard

Simple, quick, and effective — get protected in under a minute.

1

Shake Well

Before every use, shake the bottle for 5–10 seconds to ensure all natural ingredients are evenly blended.

2

Dispense on Palm

Pour 2–3 drops into your palm or onto a cotton pad. A little goes a long way with NaturGuard.

3

Apply to Exposed Skin

Gently rub onto arms, legs, neck, and other exposed areas. Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes.

4

Reapply as Needed

For extended outdoor activities, reapply every 6–8 hours or after sweating heavily or swimming.

5

Room Diffusion

For indoor protection, add 10–15 drops to a diffuser or warm water bowl and place near windows or entry points.

6

Storage

Store in a cool, dark place below 30°C. Keep tightly sealed. Best used within 12 months of opening.

⚠️

Safety Note: Perform a patch test before first use. For children under 6 months, consult a paediatrician. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.
